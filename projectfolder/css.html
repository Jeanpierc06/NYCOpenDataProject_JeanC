Filtering NYC pool inspections

Home Page Information Analysis

this page basically dumps a huge list of inspection records from NYC Open Data. You can filter the data by things like the number of violations, the Accela ID, or the permit type. After that, the page shows tons of entries with details like whether the pool is indoor or outdoor, the address, the date of inspection, what type of inspection it was (routine, complaint, re‑inspection, etc.), how many violations were found, and even the latitude/longitude. It’s basically a giant dataset turned into a webpage so you can search through pool inspection history without digging through raw data yourself.

Accela is a government technology company that provides a cloud-based platform for managing permits, licensing, inspections, and other civic services.
